About

What is the compliance checker?
The compliance checker is a tool to check the compliance of of netcdf/.nc files, allowing you to browse/drag a local file from your computer, or entering in a url to a .nc file. The data will be checked against the compliance selected from the dropdown, with options for ACDD 1.3, ACDD 1.1, CF 1.6, IOOS 1.1, IOOS 0.1, and IOOS SOS 0.1.

What does the compliance checker check?
The compliance checker checks for High, Medium, and Low priority issues with standard names/keywords and provides a score and corrective action suggestions for fixing compliance problems that are detected.
